BEHIND THE SCENES

CHOOSE

the right game

The application features a variety of motorsports and games for you to choose from. Explore the latest upcoming races that you can bet on.


When choosing a game, assess the game's scoring criteria and prize distribution strategies. Some games are simple, while others are more advanced. You can also check the current number of participants and the pool prizes to calculate your winning probabilities.

PICK

the best options.

Found a game you're confident about? Learn the exact details of a race and its drivers before you place a bet.


Each driver is allotted points for their performance during a race. Assess their past performance and make choices that are in alignment with the scoring criteria. Choose the right amount that suits your confidence.

TRACK

your progress.

Metrics continue to change throughout a game. Stay updated after a race begins and ends through the app.


Once a race is concluded, final driver points are calculated and released. Each user's choices allot them a rank, determining the segment of the distribution criteria you fall in, followed by the distribution of YOUR WINS!
